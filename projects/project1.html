Emoji Guessing Game

I created a "hangman" style game using CSS and vanilla JavaScript. The player can guess what the TV/Movie is, based on the string of emojis presented at the top of the screen.


I used a key/value pair to randomly pull from. If the player correctly guesses, they'll see the letter they selected turn green. If they guessed incorrectly, the letter will shake and turn red. They'll also lose a heart for each incorrect guess.
